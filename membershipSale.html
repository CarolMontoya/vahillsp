Details

Share Purchase

The Virginia Hills Club, Inc. is owned by its members, who are the shareholders in the Club. To become a member, either a family or single, pay a one-time fee to purchase a share. That fee $350 for the 2006 season. New shareholders can pay this amount all at once or make a $50 down payment when they join, with the rest paid in increments through the Club's easy payment plan. Payment can be made by check or credit card via Paypal.

Shareholders who terminate their membership by informing the Club in writing and requesting a buy-back are eligible to have 85% of their share price returned to them at a time approved by the Club's Board.

Seasonal Dues

Shareholders also pay dues before the start of every season, which should be paid by May 1 every year. Payments may be spaced out in advance of that date. New members should also pay seasonal dues by May 1 or as soon as they join. New members whose households include extended family members should discuss special arrangements with the Membership Committee representatives.

Two classes of associate (non-shareholding) memberships are available. Associate Members pay a single-season fee of $650. If you anticipate being in the area or using the pool for more than one season, however, it makes sense to become a shareholder from the beginning. Associate membership fees are likely to increase in future seasons.

For Military Personnel

The Virginia Hills Club also welcomes military families, who only may be in our community for a few years. We have created a special type of Associate Membership, the Active Duty Military Membership, which costs $450 per season. This benefit can be used for two consecutive seasons. If a duty tour is extended or is expected to last for more than two swim seasons, active duty personnel are encouraged to consider becoming shareholders. Special consideration will be given when such military shareholders rotate out of the area.
