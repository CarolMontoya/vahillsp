    EVENTS at the POOL

While Virginia Hills Pool prides itself in providing an atmosphere for relaxation, it also offers a broad range of activities for its members:
  • Swim lessons for children and water aerobics for adults.
  • Swim team activities for children ages 6 to 18 years of age.
  • Special parties and cookouts for adults, families and teenagers. Book your own party at the pool!
  • Raft nights -- bring your inflatables and have fun in the water from 6 - 9 p.m. on Wednesdays in July and August.

A tentative list of special activities planned for the 2006 season follows. Notices will be placed at the pool and e-mails sent to members during the season confirming these events:
