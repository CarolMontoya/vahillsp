    ACTIVITIES at the POOL

While Virginia Hills Pool prides itself in providing an atmosphere for relaxation, it also offers a broad range of activities for its members:
  • Swim lessons for children and water aerobics for adults.
  • Swim team activities for children ages 6 to 18 years of age.
  • Special parties and cookouts for adults, families and teenagers.
  • Raft nights -- bring your inflatables and have fun in the water from 6 - 9 p.m. on Wednesdays in July and August.

A tentative list of special activities planned for the 2005 season follows. Notices will be placed at the pool and e-mails sent to members during the season confirming these events:

  • May 21 -- Open House.
  • May 28 -- Opening day for the pool.
  • June 05 -- Open House for the community.
  • June 24 -- End of School Pool Party for Kids (4 p.m.)
  • July 4 -- Pre and post fireworks picnic by Poolside.
  • July ? -- Island Night adult party.
  • September 2 - Back to school Teen Night.
  • September 5 -- Closing Day activities.
