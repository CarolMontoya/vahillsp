TIDAL WAVE

SWIM TEAM

The Virginia Hills pool swim team, the Tidal Wave, is one of the oldest teams in the 104-team Northern Virginia Swimming League (NVSL). Children between ages 5 and 18, both boys and girls, are welcome to join, even if they are not strong swimmers now. Practices begin immediately after Memorial Day after school hours at the pool. Different times are set aside for each age group. Once school ends, practices switch to weekday mornings and may include some evenings at the discretion of the coaches and adult leadership of the team. Swim meets begin on Saturday mornings in mid-June 19, and run through July. This is a fun activity that brings children of all ages together to enjoy each others' company and the camaraderie of a great team sport. For more information, visit www.gotidalwave.com or e-mail keith@gotidalwave.com.
