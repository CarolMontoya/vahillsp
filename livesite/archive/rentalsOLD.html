RENTALS

The Virginia Hills Club's pool is available for rentals. Organizations which would like to bring groups, such as daycare classes, into the pool on either a regular or one-time basis during normal pool hours or in the mornings prior to general openings, should send an e-mail to info@vahillspool.org or call the pool information number at 703-317-5900 and leave a message, stating the dates and times in which you are interested.

PARTIES

The Virginia Hills Club welcomes birthday parties, team parties and other similar events, during regular pool hours or by special arrangements at other times. Arrangements must be made in advance by contacting the Club at either info@vahillspool.org or calling 703-317-5900 and leaving a message. Use of picnic tables, the patio lounge, and access to the pool and other facilities are included. Pool members can participate in such parties at no charge. Non-members (adults and children), whether swimming or not, are asked to pay $4 per person, payable by check made out to the Virginia Hills Club in advance. Alcohol, except at Club-organized events, is prohibited.
